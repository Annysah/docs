The right provisioner depends on your operational environment.

<p>
  The JWK provisioner supports passwords and one-time tokens. It's simple and
  general purpose. To add a JWK provisioner called{' '}
  <inlineCode>{props.doc.server.name}</inlineCode> to a hosted Certificate
  Manager authority (if you haven't already), run:
</p>

<CodeBlock autoCopy language="shell">
  {`step beta ca provisioner add ${props.doc.server.name} --type JWK --create --x509-default-dur 720h`}
</CodeBlock>

For instructions on adding provisioners to open source `step-ca`, or to learn more about other provisioner types, see _[Configuring `step-ca` Provisioners](https://smallstep.com/docs/step-ca/provisioners)_.
